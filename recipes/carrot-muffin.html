Carrot muffin

Healthy carrot muffins made with whole wheat flour, Applesauce and cinnamon! They taste fantastic, too, of course. These muffins make a great, quick breakfast! Recipe yields 16 muffins.

Ingredients:

  • 238g of whole wheat flour.
  • Â½ tsp of salt.
  • 2 tsp of baking powder.
  • 2 tsp of cinnamon.
  • 2 large eggs.
  • 120g of vegetable oil.
  • 150g of unsweetened applesauce.
  • 120g of whole milk.
  • 266g of granulated sugar.
  • 180g of grated carrot.

Directions:

  1. Grate your carrots.
  2. Sift and whisk together the dry ingredients.
  3. Whisk wet ingredients.
  4. Whisk in the sugar.
  5. Stir in the grated carrot.
  6. Fold in the dry ingredients.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
